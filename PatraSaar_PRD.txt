Product Name: PatraSaar Tagline: Legal clarity, distilled.

  ---------------------
  1. PRODUCT OVERVIEW
  ---------------------

1.1 Problem Statement Indian legal documents are complex, verbose, and
difficult for non-lawyers to understand. Citizens and small businesses
struggle to interpret contracts, notices, FIRs, judgments, and policies
without expensive legal assistance.

There is no AI-first platform focused on Indian law that: - Simplifies
legal documents into plain language - Grounds explanations in authentic
legal sources - Helps users understand rights and obligations without
replacing lawyers

1.2 Solution PatraSaar is a SaaS platform powered by a robust
Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) pipeline trained on Indian legal
documents. It enables users to:

-   Declassify complex legal documents
-   Ask legal questions grounded in Indian law
-   Understand implications, risks, and next steps
-   Access legal aid guidance (non-advisory)

  -----------------
  2. TARGET USERS
  -----------------

Primary Users: - Individuals handling legal documents - Small business
owners and startups - Students and researchers - NGOs and legal aid
clinics

Secondary Users: - Junior lawyers and legal interns - Compliance teams
in MSMEs - Journalists and RTI activists

  -------------------
  3. CORE USE CASES
  -------------------

-   Upload a legal document and receive a simplified explanation
-   Clause-by-clause breakdown of contracts and notices
-   Ask contextual questions with cited legal sources
-   Classify documents by type, jurisdiction, and urgency
-   Receive guidance on typical legal processes and authorities

  -----------------
  4. KEY FEATURES
  -----------------

4.1 Document Declassification - PDF, DOCX, TXT, scanned documents -
Plain-language summaries - Clause-level explanations - Highlighted risks
and obligations

4.2 AI Legal Assistant (RAG-based) - Question answering grounded in
retrieved legal context - Mandatory citations to Acts, Sections, and
Judgments - Clear uncertainty handling

4.3 Legal Document Classification - Contract, Notice, FIR, Judgment,
Policy - Civil vs Criminal - Central vs State law relevance

4.4 Legal Aid Guidance Layer - Explains applicable laws and
authorities - Typical process flow and timelines - Indicates when to
consult a lawyer (Note: Not legal advice)

  --------------------------
  5. RAG PIPELINE OVERVIEW
  --------------------------

Data Sources: - Constitution of India - Central and State Acts - Supreme
Court and High Court judgments - Government notifications and circulars

Pipeline Steps: 1. Document ingestion and OCR 2. Legal-aware chunking
and metadata tagging 3. Embedding generation 4. Hybrid retrieval
(semantic + keyword) 5. Re-ranking for accuracy 6. Grounded response
generation with citations

  10. RISKS AND MITIGATION

  11. PRODUCT VISION
  --------------------

PatraSaar is not a lawyer. It is a legal comprehension platform that
